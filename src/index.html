About Us

River Heights Intermediate School FBLA is a student-run organization passionate in providing leadership and career-oriented skills to its community. Every member has the opportunity to attend conferences, participate in fun meetings and events, attend workshops from business and community experts, and much more. Explore what RHIS FBLA has to offer.

Conferences

FBLA offers conferences and competitions that every member can participate in. RHIS FBLA competes at the Section Leadership Conference, the State Leadership Conference, and the National Leadership Conference. At these conferences, members attend workshops led by industry professionals, compete in a multitude of events, network with like-minded students and workshop presenters, and earn recognition for their efforts (which stand out on college applications).

Community Service (Work in progress)

RHIS FBLA provides local volunteering opportunities for our members to get more community service hours and to provide for their community. These opportunities can be anything, from street cleanups to food pantry packing. Community Service opportunities are released when more are found, being provided on a monthly basis.
