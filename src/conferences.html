Conferences

What are conferences?

Conferences are events hosted by the section, state, and national levels of FBLA. Members have the opportunity to learn valuable life skills that they can readily apply from these conferences. Conferences offer professional workshops, competitive events, an expo hall full of amazing sponsors, and the opportunity to network with students and professionals from all over the region, state, and nation.

Compete

Compete in over 12+ competitive events with topics that cater to almost all interests like business, technology, finance, and career readiness. Every competitor has the opportunity to compete for awards and recognition.

Learn

Attend workshops and presentations from business professionals and keynote speakers and learn important skills and topics such as presenting, mindset, emotional intelligence, and ethics.

Network

Meet with like-minded students from all over the section, state, and nation, and create friendships with students and presenters at every conference.
